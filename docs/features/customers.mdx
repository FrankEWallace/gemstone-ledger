title: Customers
description: Customer profiles, per-customer expense breakdowns, and customer reports.
---

The Customers module manages the people and businesses you sell to.

## Customer profiles

Each customer record includes:

- Name, contact details, and notes
- Linked transaction history (income entries referencing this customer)
- Per-customer expense breakdown

## Per-customer reports

From any customer profile, export a PDF report showing:

- Total revenue from this customer over a selected period
- Transaction-by-transaction history
- Expense breakdown attributed to this customer

## Linking transactions

When creating an income transaction, optionally link it to a customer. This populates the customer's history and enables per-customer reporting.
