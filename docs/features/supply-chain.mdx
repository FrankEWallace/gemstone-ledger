title: Supply Chain
description: Suppliers, sales channels, and purchase orders.
---

The Supply Chain module manages the inbound side of your operations — who you buy from and how you purchase.

## Suppliers

Maintain a directory of suppliers with contact details and notes. Each supplier can be linked to purchase orders.

## Sales channels

Track the channels through which you sell output (direct buyers, auction houses, brokers, etc.). Sales channel data feeds into income reporting.

## Purchase orders

Log purchase orders against suppliers. Each order records:

- Supplier
- Items ordered and quantities
- Expected delivery date
- Status (pending / received / cancelled)

Marking an order as **received** can optionally update inventory quantities for the items received.
