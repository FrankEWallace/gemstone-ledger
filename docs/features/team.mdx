The Team module manages your workforce — who works for you, when they work, and how many hours they've logged.

## Staff directory

Each team member record includes:

- Name, role, and contact details
- Employment type and status
- Notes

## Shift scheduling

Plan shifts by assigning team members to specific dates and times. The schedule view shows the week at a glance.

## Timesheets

Log actual hours worked per team member. Timesheets feed into payroll reporting and help reconcile planned vs. actual labour.

## Roles

Team members are assigned an application role (Admin, Manager, Viewer, etc.) that controls what they can see and do within Gemstone Ledger. See [Roles & Permissions](/administration/roles-permissions) for details.
