---
title: Equipment
description: Equipment registry and maintenance tracking.
---

The Equipment module maintains a registry of all physical assets used in operations and tracks their maintenance history.

## Equipment registry

Each asset record includes:

- Name, type, and serial number
- Purchase date and cost
- Status (operational / under maintenance / retired)
- Notes

## Maintenance logs

Log maintenance events against any equipment item. Each log entry records:

- Date of maintenance
- Type (scheduled / repair / inspection)
- Description of work performed
- Cost
- Next scheduled maintenance date

## Alerts

If a next-maintenance date passes without a new log entry, an alert is raised. Configure maintenance alert rules in [Alert Rules](/features/alerts).
