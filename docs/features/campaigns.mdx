---
title: Campaigns
description: Org-wide operational campaigns targeted at specific sites.
---

Campaigns are org-level initiatives — a safety drive, an equipment inspection push, a production sprint — that can target one or more of your sites. Unlike most modules, campaigns belong to the **organization**, not a single site, so the page shows the same list regardless of the active site.

## Creating a campaign

Each campaign has:

- **Title** and optional description
- **Start and end dates** (optional)
- **Target sites** — pick which of your sites the campaign applies to, or leave empty for all

New campaigns start in **Draft** status.

## Status workflow

Campaigns move through three states:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Draft** | Being planned — visible but not yet running |
| **Active** | In progress (use the play action to activate) |
| **Completed** | Finished (use the complete action to close it out) |

Campaigns can also be deleted; deletion is permanent.

## Enabling the module

Campaigns is a toggleable module. Org owners/admins can turn it on or off for the whole organization from **Settings → System → Module Configuration**. When disabled it disappears from the sidebar for everyone.
