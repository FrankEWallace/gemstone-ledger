The Campaigns module tracks marketing and sales campaigns — what you ran, when, and how it performed.

## Creating a campaign

Each campaign record includes:

- **Name** and description
- **Start and end dates**
- **Budget** allocated
- **Status** — Draft / Active / Completed / Cancelled
- **Notes** and outcome summary

## Performance tracking

Link income transactions to a campaign to track revenue attributed to it. Compare spend (budget) against attributed revenue to evaluate ROI.

## Status workflow

Campaigns move from Draft → Active → Completed as they progress. Cancelled campaigns are preserved for historical reference.
