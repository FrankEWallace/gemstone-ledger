The Safety module records all safety-related events on site and tracks their resolution.

## Logging an incident

Each incident record captures:

- **Date and time**
- **Severity** — Minor / Moderate / Serious / Critical
- **Description** of what occurred
- **Individuals involved**
- **Location**
- **Immediate actions taken**

## Resolution tracking

Incidents move through a workflow:

1. **Open** — logged but unresolved
2. **In progress** — investigation or corrective action underway
3. **Resolved** — corrective action completed, notes recorded

Admins can mark incidents resolved and add resolution notes. Open critical incidents surface as alerts on the Dashboard.

## Safety log

The full chronological log of all incidents is filterable by severity, date range, and status. Export the log as a report from the [Reports](/features/reports) module.
