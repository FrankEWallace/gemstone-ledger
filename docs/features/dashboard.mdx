title: Dashboard

The Dashboard is the landing screen after login. It provides a live summary of your organisation's key performance indicators across all operational areas.

## KPI cards

The top of the dashboard shows stat cards for:

- **Revenue** — total income for the selected period
- **Expenses** — total outgoings for the selected period
- **Production** — units produced in the selected period
- **Inventory value** — current stock value on hand

Each card shows a period-over-period comparison and trend direction.

## Activity feed

A chronological feed of recent activity across all modules (new transactions, production entries, incidents, etc.) gives a quick pulse on operations without navigating to each module.

## Alert banner

Active alert rules that have breached their thresholds surface here. Click any alert to navigate directly to the relevant module.

## Period selector

All KPIs respond to the global period selector (day / week / month / quarter / year). The selection persists per session.
